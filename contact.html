Frequently Asked Questions

How long does game development take?

Development time varies based on project scope and complexity. Simple mobile games may take 2-3 months, while complex projects can take 6-12 months or more.

What platforms do you develop for?

We develop games for Android (Play Store), PC (Steam), and web platforms. We can also discuss iOS development based on your requirements.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! We offer ongoing support, bug fixes, updates, and feature additions after your game launches.

Can you help with publishing?

Absolutely! We assist with the entire publishing process including store setup, optimization, and submission to Play Store, Steam, and other platforms.
